Frequently Asked Questions

You can schedule a donation pickup by filling out our donation form for the specific type of donation (food, clothing, books, etc.). Select "Pickup from my location" as your preferred method and choose a convenient date and time slot. Our team will confirm the pickup details via email.

If you need to reschedule a pickup, please contact our support team at least 24 hours before the scheduled time. You can reach us at support@unityserve.org or call +91-863-036-0001. We'll work with you to find a new time that's convenient for both you and our volunteer team.

Please prepare your items in the following way to ensure a smooth pickup:

  • Food Donations: Pack non-perishable items in a sturdy box or bag. For perishable items, ensure they're properly refrigerated until pickup.
  • Clothing Donations: Clean and fold clothes, placing them in bags or boxes.
  • Book Donations: Pack books in manageable-sized boxes (not too heavy).
  • Label boxes/bags with the type of donation.
  • If possible, place the items near your entrance for easy access.

After your donation is collected and distributed, we'll send you an impact report via email. This report includes details about which communities or organizations received your donation and how it was used to help those in need. We believe in transparency and want you to see the positive impact of your generosity.

Yes, all donations are eligible for tax deductions. Once your donation is collected, we'll send you a tax receipt via email. If you need a physical copy, please mention this in the "Additional Notes" section when scheduling your pickup, and our volunteer will bring a printed receipt when collecting your donation.

Need help with your donation pickup?

Our support team is ready to assist you with any questions or concerns about your donation pickup. Contact us via phone, email, or the chat feature on our website.
